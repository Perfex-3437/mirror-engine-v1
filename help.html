Exploring the Agent Network

This space is designed for exploration, not judgment. You are observing how agents interact, influence one another, and adapt under uncertainty.

What you’re seeing

  • Nodes represent agents (attacker, defender, observer).
  • Edges represent possible influence or attack paths.
  • Uncertainty means lack of confidence, not danger.

What to experiment with

  • Add nodes → see how uncertainty spreads.
  • Trigger probes → simulate investigation.
  • Pause and step → watch decision effects.

Reflective questions

Which agents gain influence as uncertainty increases, and why?
What assumptions are you making about intent when you label a node as an attacker or defender?
How does adding a single observer change the behavior of the entire network?
Where does uncertainty amplify insight rather than confusion?
If you remove the idea of a "correct" outcome, what patterns become easier to notice?

There is no correct outcome. You are exploring patterns, not answers.
